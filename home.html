Expensify is here to help your business succeed every step of the way and provide valuable insight into your business's spending patterns.

Expensify products and services

Expensify directly connects to accounting, HR, payroll, and travel software, giving companies real-time visibility and control over their business spend.


Learn more below about some of our services and how Expensify can help your business shine.

Expense management

Automate expense reporting, set up next-day reimbursements, and sync with accounting software, all with our expense management service.

Corporate card

Our free corporate card allows for faster expense reporting, making reconciliation a breeze. Plus, get 1% cash back on every US purchase and 2% back if you spend $250k+/month across cards.

Spend management

Manage spend reports, vendor bills, payment records, budget assignments, and more with our spend management service.

Expense reports

We'll do your expense reports for you, so you can focus on what really matters for your business.

Bill Pay

Send your vendor bills to Expensify for automatic tracking, approval, and payment through our bill pay feature.

Scan Receipts

Automatically upload and import receipts in one click, thanks to our state-of-the-art receipt scanning feature.

Invoices

With our invoicing software, you can create and send invoices, collect payments, and easily sync to your accounting package.

Personal Payments

Split bills, request payments, and chat with friends. Give it a go at new.expensify.com!

Travel

Book flights, hotels, and cars simply by chatting with the Expensify travel Concierge.

Global reimbursement

Easily reimburse employees no matter where they are or what currency they use with our global reimbursement feature.

Virtual cards

Make purchases without a physical card on hand by tapping your virtual Expensify Card.

Financial chat

Consider Expensify Chat your hub for financial convos with colleagues, clients, and friends. Get started on New Expensify today.

Is Expensify free to use?

Expensify is free to use for personal spending, and some services, such as the Expensify Card, are also free to use. However, if you want to upgrade your plan to include account integrations, custom reporting, and more, that does come with a small fee.
